SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to.

    Ingredient

  1. Lemon
  2. Icing Sugar
  3. Sugar
  4. Pinch of salt
  5. Unsalted Butter
  6. Self-raising Flour
  7. Eggs
  8. Backing Powder

Equipment

  • Cake tin
  • Weighing scale
  • Smaller bowls,to hold ingredients
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Grater
